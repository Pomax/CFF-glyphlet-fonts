OpenType discussion forum

An e-mail based discussion forum has been set up specifically to help people working on the development of OpenType fonts and associated technologies.

  • To subscribe to the OpenType Mailing List, send e-mail to: subscribe-opentype@indx.co.uk
  • To remove yourself from this mailing list, send e-mail to: unsubscribe-opentype@indx.co.uk

The list moderator has adopted a proactive subscription method, which means that he checks all requests to subscribe prior to approving them. This necessarily means that subscription requests can take some time to process. Requests to unsubscribe are automated.

Please note that if you join the forum your e-mail address will be accessible to the other subscribers.
